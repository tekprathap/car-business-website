Frequently Asked Questions

What types of cars are available for rent?

We offer a wide variety of cars, including sedans, SUVs, and luxury cars. Choose the one that fits your needs!

Do I need insurance to rent a car?

Yes, we provide insurance options during the booking process. You can choose the coverage that suits your needs.

What are the payment options available?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and online payment options like PayPal for your convenience.

Can I modify or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the rental period starts. Cancellation fees may apply.

What is the minimum age to rent a car?

The minimum age to rent a car is 21, and a valid driving license is required. Additional fees may apply for drivers under 25.
